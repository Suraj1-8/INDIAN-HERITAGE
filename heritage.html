India's Heritage Sites

Explore the ancient monuments, temples, and architectural wonders that tell the story of India's glorious past

UNESCO World Heritage Sites

Cultural Heritage Sites

  • Taj Mahal

    A stunning white marble mausoleum in Agra, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

  • Red Fort

    A historic fort in Delhi that served as the main residence of Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

  • Khajuraho Group of Monuments

    A group of Hindu and Jain temples known for their intricate sculptures and architectural excellence.

  • Hampi

    The last capital of the great Vijayanagara Empire, featuring stunning ruins and temples.

Natural Heritage Sites

  • Kaziranga National Park

    Home to the world's largest population of one-horned rhinoceroses and diverse wildlife.

  • Sundarbans

    The largest mangrove forest in the world, home to the Bengal tiger and diverse marine life.

  • Western Ghats

    A mountain range with exceptional biodiversity and unique ecosystems.

  • Great Himalayan National Park

    A protected area in the western Himalayas with diverse flora and fauna.

Heritage Conservation

Conservation Efforts

India has implemented various conservation programs to protect its heritage sites from natural decay, pollution, and human activities.

  • Archaeological Survey of India (ASI)

    The primary organization responsible for the conservation and maintenance of heritage sites.

  • Adopt a Heritage Scheme

    A government initiative to involve private and public sector companies in the maintenance and development of heritage sites.

  • Digital Documentation

    Using 3D scanning and digital mapping to create detailed records of heritage structures for future conservation.

How You Can Help

There are several ways you can contribute to the preservation of India's heritage:

  • Responsible Tourism

    Follow guidelines, avoid touching artifacts, and respect the sanctity of heritage sites.

  • Volunteer Programs

    Participate in conservation programs and awareness campaigns organized by heritage organizations.

  • Support Heritage Organizations

    Donate to organizations working towards heritage conservation and restoration.
